MapartWall is a website that shows all the maps currently on 2b2t
This is a project with the intention of discouraging map resets due to people wanting to build their own map walls, as well as just a cool site for peole to browse around on
Note: All of the images on this site are unmoderated and copied over straight from the Minecraft server - this might include nsfw and otherwise uncomfortable or offensive content, viewer discretion is advised
